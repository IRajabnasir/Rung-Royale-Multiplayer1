## Long description (Google Play: 4000 chars)

**Rung Royale — the 4-player trick-taking card game reimagined.**

Also known as Court Piece, Rang, or Coat Piece — Rung is a classic partnership card game played across South Asia. Rung Royale brings it to your phone with clean modern design, smart AI opponents, and global multiplayer.

**FEATURES**

• **Single-player Practice** — Three AI difficulty levels. Our Medium and Hard AI actually play strategically: they count cards, know when to trump, protect their partner, and decide when to secure vs go for the court.

• **Online Arena** — Public matches with players worldwide. Jump in instantly or create your own lobby.

• **Ranked Battles** — Climb the trophy ladder. Wins count, losses hurt. Your permanent rank shows on your profile.

• **Private Rooms** — 6-character match codes. Share with friends, play together, no strangers.

• **Court / Super Court** — Play the full Rung ruleset including securing tricks, going for Court (win all 7 needed), and Super Court (opponents go blank).

• **Ace-Off Variant** — Toggle between classic (Ace high) and Ace-Off (Ace low) for traditional regional play.

• **Multiple Themes** — 10 board themes from classic felt to cyber neon, sakura, night-scope, and more.

• **Stats & Progression** — Track wins, sets, courts, super courts, win streaks, trophies, and XP. Earn badges for milestones like your first Super Court or a 5-win streak.

• **Clean native feel** — Haptic feedback on every card play, full-screen immersive gameplay, auto-rotating landscape mode for the table.

Rung Royale is free to play, with no forced ads and no pay-to-win. Built by card-game enthusiasts, for card-game enthusiasts.

Ready to rung? Drop in.
